Hartley Wintney Football Club

HWFC Summary

Hartley Wintney Football Club is a semi-professional football club that was founded in 1897 and plays in the Isthmian League in South England.

HWFC Players

Hartley Wintney have a squad of 16 players that have an average age of 24 years old.

Danny Ackland is the first team manager for Hartley Wintney Football Club. He has been coaching for over 20 years and previously a professional footballer for Charlton Athletic and AFC Bournemouth.

Anthony Millerick is the Director of Football for Hartley Wintney Football Club but was also previously the first team manager. He achieved two league titles, a play-off win and two promotions over his tenure at the club before stepping down as first team manager.

Hartley Wintney Junior Football Club has a range of both boys and girls teams going from ages 6 all the way up to an under 23s team. It has more Junior teams than almost all other clubs in Hampshire with and average of 2 or 3 teams for each age group.
